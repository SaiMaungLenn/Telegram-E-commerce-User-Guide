For Users (Customers)

Main Menu & Navigation

Start with /start. Easily navigate through Shopping, Promotions, Search, Cart, Orders, and your Profile from one central menu.

Browsing & Shopping

Explore products by category. View detailed product pages with photos, descriptions, and prices. Use the âž• and âž– buttons to select your desired quantity.

Cart & Checkout

Add items to your cart or use 'Buy Now' to proceed directly. The bot checks if your phone and address are complete before you select a payment method.

Payment & Proof

After selecting a payment method, you'll see payment details and a QR code. After paying, press 'Transferred' and send your payment screenshot as proof.

Profile & Settings

Manage your contact info and shipping address in your Profile. In Settings, switch the language, view your affiliate stats, or read the T&C.

Orders & Support

Track your order history and status in 'My Orders'. For any help, use the 'Support' button to get contact information for the shop admin.

For Admins (Shop Owners)

Admin Panel 1: Operations

  • Manage Orders: View pending orders, see customer payment proof, and approve or reject payments with a single tap. Customers are notified automatically.
  • Broadcast Message: Send announcements or marketing messages (text, photos, videos) to all of your bot's users at once.
  • Update Content: Easily update the content for your 'Promotions' and 'News' sections by sending new messages to the bot.
  • Customize Appearance: Update all captions and poster photos for every section to match your brand's unique style.
  • Manage Admins & Users: Add or remove other admins. View and block/unblock users from sending support messages.

Admin Panel 2: Shop Setup

  • Add Category & Product: A guided, step-by-step flow to add new categories and products, including name, price, description, and media.
  • Set Currency: Before adding your first product, you must set your shop's currency (e.g., MMK, USD). This is a one-time, permanent setting.
  • Manage Payments: Add or remove payment methods your customers can use. Provide a name, number, and an optional QR code for each.
  • Delete Content: Safely delete products or entire categories. Deleting a category will also delete all products within it, with a confirmation prompt.
  • Custom Commands: Create unique slash commands (e.g., /aboutus, /policy) and define the content the bot should reply with.

Admin Commands

  • /statistics - Get a real-time, detailed report of your shop's performance, including revenue, order counts, user growth, and top-selling products.
  • /myusers - See a list of all users who joined the bot via your referral link, and view the bots they have created on the platform.

How to get your own E-commerce Bot?

  1. 1. Go to @BotFather on Telegram.
  2. 2. Send the /newbot command and follow the instructions.
  3. 3. Provide a display name for your bot (e.g., "My Awesome Shop").
  4. 4. Provide a unique username that must end with 'bot' (e.g., MyAwesomeShopBot). Special characters are not allowed.
  5. 5. Once created, @BotFather will give you an API Token. Tap the token to copy it.
  6. 6. Go to the official bot creator: @tg_ecommerce_official_bot.
  7. 7. Press the 'Create Your Bot' button.
  8. 8. Paste and send the API Token you copied from @BotFather.
